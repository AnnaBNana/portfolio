About Me

Hi there! My name is Anna. I'm a web engineer and an educator. I work at Coding Dojo, a unique fast-paced 14-week bootcamp where students learn three full web and/or mobile stacks. I'm passionate about teaching, learning, and new technologies!

I'm a big fan of Python, and I like to dabble in JavaScript from time to time. I've built some stuff you can look at in Python with Django or Flask, Node.js and Swift. The mobile apps aren't quite ready for prime-time yet, but I'm spending 2018 getting better at mobile app development.

I might be a giant nerd, but I do other stuff, too. I have a horse and am very close with my family. I like spending my free time with the people and animals I love the most. In my previous professional life I've worked a lot with animals.

As for my web development qualifications, I'll let my work speak for itself.
